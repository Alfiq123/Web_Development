Minecraft Mobs

Zombie
Zombies are common undead hostile mobs that deal melee damage and drop rotten flesh on death.
Skeleton
A skeleton is an undead hostile mob that performs ranged attacks with a bow.
Creeper
A creeper is a common hostile mob that quietly approaches players and then explodes, which can destroy blocks and deal massive amounts of damage. Creepers are a major source of gunpowder as well as the only way to obtain most music discs.
Enderman
An enderman is a tall neutral mob found in all three dimensions. It becomes hostile to players that damage it or look directly at its face, using its arms to attack. Endermen teleport erratically, as well as away from dangers such as lava, projectiles, and water. They also occasionally pick up and move certain blocks.
