Basic White Bread Recipe

Yields: 1 loaf
Prep time: 30 minutes
Rise time: 1-2 hours
Bake time: 30-40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (105-115°F)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast: In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5-10 minutes, or until foamy.
  2. Combine dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together salt and flour.
  3. Add wet ingredients: Pour the activated yeast mixture and olive oil into the dry ingredients. Stir until a dough forms.
  4. Knead: Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  5. Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (about 1-2 hours).
  6. Shape: Punch down the dough and shape into a loaf. Place it in a greased loaf pan.
  7. Second rise: Cover the loaf and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Bake: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the bread for 30-40 minutes, or until golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.
  9. Cool: Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, use whole wheat flour or a combination of all-purpose and whole wheat flour.
  • Add herbs or spices like rosemary, thyme, or garlic powder for extra flavor.
  • For a crispier crust, bake the bread on a pizza stone or baking sheet.

Enjoy your homemade bread!
